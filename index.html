About

India Arts

INDIA ARTS is a premier die-making company, established in the year 1988, by Mr. Yakub Shaikh which is Committed to quality, performance & meeting customer's deadlines.

INDIA ARTS is proud to be a market-oriented & professionally managed Company, having acquired the most sophisticated computerized solution by way of Auto Cad and other software.

This helps in the creation of quicker designs more and flexible options within no time resulting in faster customer review as well as final approvals of the die.

In today's competitive business environment qualified, trained & customer-oriented manpower gives a cutting edge to service a broad customer base in public and private sectors.
